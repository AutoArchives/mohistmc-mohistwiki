## Generate patches
---

> **Before starting, be sure you followed the [setup tutorial](./setup.md).**

### Steps
* Generate patches
  * Linux:    
     `./gradlew genPatches`
  * Windows:      
     `gradlew.bat genPatches`

When it's done, you can go and have a cup of tea, it can take a long time (allow up to 5 minutes).
